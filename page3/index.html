The Eminence in Shadow

The Eminence in Shadow is an isekai where the main character, who was in search of strength to the point of insanity, obtains magic. There are many characters, all of which have varied backgrounds and traits.

I personally love this anime because the main character just doesn't care about anything but accomplishes many things. He also plays the role of a random guy, using his power to enhance his background-characterness. Overall, I recommend EVERYONE, including YOU to watch it. Go. Go watch it. Now. I'm not asking.
